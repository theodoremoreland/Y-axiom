Humidity

It's difficult to derive significance from this visualization alone. Initial impressions would likely relate to the consistency of 100% humidity values and the small clustering of 0% humidity in cities located between 60-80 degrees latitude. What can be inferred, however, is that cities trend towards 65% and 100% humidity, regardless of latitudinal position.
