Summary


The WeatherPy project tasked us with substantiating a well known fact: the weather gets warmer as you move towards the equator. In order to accomplish this, we used Python requests, APIs, JSON traversals, and Matplotlib to retrieve, analyze, and visualize our data. What we got was a series of scatter plots illustrating the latitudinal relationships of temperature, cloudiness, humidity, and wind speed ranging across 500 cities.

This dashboard features each of the four illustrations with supplemental explanations, as well as a table featuring all of the data used to conduct the analysis. To access an illustration, click on one of the images on the right (or below), or select an image from the "Plots" drop-down tab in the upper right-hand corner.
